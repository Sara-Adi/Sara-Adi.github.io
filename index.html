Who Am I

My Story


Hi! My name is Sara Adi, your regular (not really) 18-year-old student studying software engineering at the University of Guelph. I am a dedicated, hard-working individual high on self-efficacy. I find joy in the little things and set smaller, more achievable goals to keep my morale up.

In school, I am an executive for the University of Guelphs’ School of Computing and Informational Sciences, while also humbling myself in community volunteering. Although I am very school and work-oriented, I try (as much as possible) to enjoy the many experiences life has to offer. For example, playing team-oriented sports and working out have been high on my priority list throughout grade school, and are a great way to alleviate some stress while meeting new people. Furthermore, I love getting involved with the community and putting myself out there to network with people from all different backgrounds and groups (i.e. being an executive of the MYAC and working closely with the mayor to plan community events).

I also love spending my free time challenging my skills and creating and investing in learning opportunities. However, a passion project of mine is to start a Youtube channel to help teach other coders of all experiences and expertise the fundamental (and advance) content in the world of computer science. Asides software, I also am a passionate advocate for Mental Health, and believe it is time society takes this seriously (in all genders), and that we must adapt and configure the structure of our lives (whether it be school, work, family etc) to prioritize our MH first.

My family and close friends are my motivators, and every day they drive me to push myself further and not accept anything less. My favourite pastime is ordering a pizza and putting on a good movie with the company of loved ones.
