About The Company

Tulip Retail is a mid-sized technology startup founded in 2013 by Ali Asaria and a team of mobile commerce experts. They had one goal in mind: reinvent retail and shape the future of the in-store shopping experience. Not only did they excel at their goal, but they continue to surpass every achievement they set. Tulip has built a retail mobile software platform that empowers leading retailers such as Mulberry, Chanel, Saks Fifth Avenue, Kate Spade, Coach, and Michael Kors to give mobile devices to their store associates so they can elevate service, sell more and provide a personalized experience.

Product Delivery

The Product Delivery team at Tulip has a wide range of responsibilities. Although my time at tulip was short, I most definitely was exposed to a magnitude of opportunities to cover a wide range of obligations the team has to deliver on.

The opportunity that struck me the most would be the work I completed for a client that required the development and implementation of a full order hook, that would be integrated from Tulips system and their database. Although the work itself seemed to be standard at first, the authorization method requested was one that Tulip did not implement in any other client previously. Although this imposed a challenge, it also opened a door to learning more about AWS. I was able to expose myself to professionally written documentation from Amazon directly, which taught me the importance of documentation, how to read it, take away necessary information, and implement it into a solution.

I completed the entire hook by using Javascript and Node JS while implementing multiple external and internal libraries. Furthermore, I developed test cases for each component of the hook to ensure no faulty/undetected code was executed. This opportunity was one of many I got the privilege of experiencing at Tulip, and I truly believe it strengthened me as a developer.
